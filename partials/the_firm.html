OUR FIRM VALUES:

1 - Quality

We seek to render the finest legal services to our clients, nationally and internationally. Our attorneys undertake rigorous legal education, and participate in international conference, seminars and symposia !

2 - Teamwork

We begin by listening to the client to understand needs and objectives. Our approach emphasizes teamwork in developing careful factual and legal analyses. Each client receives the full benefit of our experience, diligence, foresight, confidentiality and loyalty.

3 - Confidentiality

We strive to maintain the complete confidentiality of our clients' affairs (Since the internet is NOT confidential, we do not render advice, nor deal with our clients' confidential matters, via e-mail or the internet).

4 - International Services

Our attorneys' backgrounds include not only advanced legal degrees and enhanced knowledge in different legal areas, but also international and foreign language or qualifications. We consider these educational & linguistic characteristics important to comprehend other jurisdictions' practices and laws, to deal effectively with international legal matters, and to efficiently service clients in their own language.


 5 - Strong Relationships

We maintain excellent relationships with renowned legal, fiduciary, banking, trust and accounting firms throughout Europe, North America as well as world-wide. Our attorneys travel often in the appropriate jurisdictions in order to ensure maximum confidentiality as well as personal relations with our clients and direct relations with their local advisors. We strengthen our teams with network expertise.
